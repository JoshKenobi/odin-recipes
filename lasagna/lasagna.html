Lasagna Recipe!

Description

To make lasagna you will need the pasta, seasoned meat and cheese

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • marinara sauce

Steps!

  1. Put pasta in boiling water
  2. Prepare the meat
  3. make a layer of pasta in your bowl
  4. Put meat on top of pasta
  5. put cheese on top of meat
  6. Add another layer of pasta
  7. Put meat on top of second layer
  8. put cheese on top of meat
  9. add another layer of pasta
  10. Add marinara sauce on top of pasta
  11. Add cheese on top of marinara
  12. put in oven
